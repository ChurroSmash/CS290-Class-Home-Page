Nair is one of Ridley's best tools while players are in neutral. It's very important for Ridley to focus on spacing and keep your opponent away from you because Ridley is very easily combo'd due to his large size. Use his huge attacks to your advantage!

Ridley has a devestating grab when the opponent is at low damage. Using his Dthrow can easily combo into Fair. Grabing the opponent in order to beat their shield is very important in Smash, so make sure you utilize this.

Ridley also has a very strong punish capability. All of his Smash Attacks are very powerful, so they can K.O. the opponent at very low damage. If you ever notice an easy opportunity to punish your opponent, like if they attack you with poor spacing, try hitting them with a Smash Attack!
